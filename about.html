About Us

This is what We do.

WELCOME to the Cordillera Region, foreign tourists and local travelers alike!

This blog is aimed at showing you the people, places, things and events that you should not miss when you are in the Cordillera Region. Let us be your reference when you open the site for the Cordillera; we will be full of information and guide for you!

Because we have so many things to show you, we are trying to go by province, so that we don’t miss a thing. We started with Benguet and then we are going around the provinces in the Cordillera. After that, we will meticulously show you the different provinces that make up the different towns in it , and to promote our local and very own tourist spots.

So go with us on this cyber trip, as we go around various places and show you our beautiful place, THE CORDILLERA!
